Legal FAQ

help_outline What happens to my data if I cancel?

Upon termination, you have a 30-day grace period to export all infrastructure configurations and data sets. After this window, EternaCloud initiates a secure cryptographic erasure of all hosted resources.

update How often are the Terms updated?

We review our legal protocols quarterly. Major updates are communicated via email 30 days prior to implementation. Minor clerical changes are logged in our public documentation repository.

verified_user Is EternaCloud GDPR compliant?

Yes. We act as a Data Processor under GDPR guidelines. Our architecture is designed for full compliance, including the right to erasure, data portability, and strict cross-border transfer protocols.

description Where can I find the DPA?

Our Data Processing Addendum (DPA) is available within the 'Global Support' section of our dashboard or can be requested directly from our compliance team via legal@eternacloud.io.
